# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail consumer shopping behavior to help businesses understand customer purchasing patterns, improve engagement, optimize marketing strategies, and increase customer loyalty.

Using Python, SQL, and Power BI, the project explores how demographics, discounts, reviews, seasons, payment methods, and sales channels influence consumer decisions and repeat purchases.

---

## Business Problem Statement

A leading retail company wants to better understand its customers’ shopping behavior in order to improve sales, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty.

The objective of this project is to answer the following business question:

> “How can the company leverage consumer shopping data to identify trends, improve customer engagement, and optimize marketing and product strategies?”

---

## Objectives

* Clean and preprocess retail consumer data
* Analyze customer purchasing patterns
* Identify key drivers behind repeat purchases
* Study the impact of discounts, seasons, reviews, and payment methods
* Build interactive dashboards for business stakeholders
* Generate actionable business recommendations

## Business Insights

Some major findings include:

* Discounts significantly influence repeat purchases
* Seasonal trends affect product demand patterns
* Online customers show different spending behavior compared to offline customers
* Customer reviews strongly impact purchasing decisions
* Certain payment methods are associated with higher-value transactions
